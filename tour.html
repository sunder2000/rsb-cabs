Tirunelveli Tourism Places

Explore famous spiritual, natural & historical destinations around Nellai with RSB Cabs.

Courtallam (Kutralam)

Known as the Spa of South India. Famous for medicinal waterfalls like Main Falls, Five Falls & Old Courtallam.

Distance: 60 KM

Papanasam

Sacred place on Thamirabarani river with Agasthiyar Falls and Papanasanathar Temple.

Distance: 45 KM

Manjolai Hills

Cool climate hill station with tea estates, Manimuthar Dam & scenic viewpoints.

Distance: 70 KM

Kalakkad Mundanthurai

Wildlife sanctuary rich in forests, waterfalls, elephants & rare species.

Distance: 50 KM

Kanyakumari

Southernmost tip of India. Vivekananda Rock, Thiruvalluvar statue & ocean views.

Distance: 85 KM

Thirparappu Falls

Beautiful waterfall surrounded by greenery. Best picnic spot for families.

Distance: 95 KM

Padmanabhapuram Palace

Historic wooden palace of Travancore kings with traditional architecture.

Distance: 90 KM

porunai museum

Museum highlighting the history, culture and civilization of the Tamirabarani river basin.

Distance: 5 KM

kaliskesham

Serene forest destination with waterfalls, greenery and rich wildlife near Western Ghats

Distance: 70 KM

Vettuvankovil

>Ancient rock-cut cave temple carved from a single stone, showcasing Pandya architecture.

Distance: 45 KM

Thenmalai

India’s first planned ecotourism destination with trekking, boating, rope bridge and adventure activities.

Distance: 60 KM
