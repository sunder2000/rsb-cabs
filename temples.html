Nellaiappar Temple – Tirunelveli

Nellaiappar Temple is one of the most ancient and magnificent Shiva temples in Tamil Nadu. Dedicated to Lord Shiva (Nellaiappar) and Goddess Kanthimathi Amman, this temple is famous for its musical pillars, massive gopurams, and spiritual vibrations. Thousands of devotees visit this temple every day seeking peace and blessings.

Tiruchendur Murugan Temple

Tiruchendur is one of the Arupadai Veedu (Six Abodes of Lord Murugan). Located near the sea, this temple is unique and powerful. Devotees believe that visiting Tiruchendur removes obstacles, fear, and negativity from life.

Aathankarai Pallivasal

Aathankarai Pallivasal is a historic mosque known for harmony and peace. People from all religions visit this place seeking blessings and mental strength.

High Court Maharaja Temple

This temple is known for its royal heritage and architectural beauty. It reflects the spiritual culture of the region and attracts devotees throughout the year.

Kulasai Mutharamman Temple

Famous for the Dasara festival, Kulasai Mutharamman Temple is a powerful Shakthi temple. Lakhs of devotees participate every year with devotion and faith.

Kanyakumari Amman Temple

Located at the southern tip of India, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Kanyakumari. The sunrise and sunset views make this place spiritually and visually divine.

Thoranamalai

Thoranamalai is known for its natural beauty and spiritual atmosphere. Devotees visit this place for meditation and peaceful moments.

Thirumalai Kovil

Thirumalai Kovil is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is surrounded by scenic hills. The calm environment gives divine experience to devotees.

Kasi Viswanathar Temple

This temple is believed to give the blessings of Kasi pilgrimage itself. Many devotees perform special poojas here for spiritual fulfillment.

Nava Thirupathi

Nava Thirupathi temples are nine sacred Vishnu temples located along the Thamirabarani river. These temples are highly significant for Vaishnavites.

Nava Kailayam

Nava Kailayam consists of nine Shiva temples representing Navagrahas. Pilgrims visit these temples for planetary relief and spiritual peace.

Manappadu Holy Cross Church

Historic and sacred church where St. Francis Xavier prayed, known for deep faith, miracles and spiritual peace.

Sorimuthu Iyyanar Kovil

Famous forest temple located near Papanasam, known for devotion, scenic nature and peaceful surroundings.

Nava kailayam Temples – Divya Desams

Nava Kailayam is a sacred group of nine Shiva temples situated along the Thamirabarani River in Tirunelveli and Thoothukudi districts. Each temple represents one of the Navagrahas (nine planets).

Devotees believe that visiting all Nava Kailayam temples removes planetary doshas, brings peace of mind, good health, and prosperity.

Important Nava Kailayam Temples

  • Papanasam – Suriyan (Sun)
  • Cheran Mahadevi – Chandran (Moon)
  • Kodaganallur – Chevvai (Mars)
  • Kunnathur – Rahu
  • Murappanadu – Guru (Jupiter)
  • Srivaikundam – Sani (Saturn)
  • Thenthiruperai – Budhan (Mercury)
  • Rajapathy – Kethu
  • Serndhapoomangalam – Sukran (Venus)

Nava Thirupathi Temples – Divya Desams

Nava Thirupathi refers to nine Divya Desams dedicated to Lord Vishnu, located in Tirunelveli district.

Pilgrims visit these temples especially during Garuda Sevai festival. Covering all nine temples in one day requires proper planning.

Important Nava Thirupathi Temples

  • Srivaikundam – Suriyan
  • Thiruvattaru – Chandran
  • Thiruppuliyangudi – Budhan
  • Irattai Thirupathi – Rahu & Kethu
  • Thenthiruperai – Sukran
  • Thirukkolur – Chevvai
  • Thirukuzhanthai – Sani
  • Azhwar Thirunagari – Vyazhan
